# Tick Tac Toe 
### Cli interface 

To start the game use : `lein run` or `clj -M -m ttt.console` 

Pick a position on the board according to the position board:

| 1   | 2   | 3   |
|-----|-----|-----|
 | 4   | 5   | 6   |
| 7   | 8   | 9   | 

=> lein run: 

Current board:

| e   | e   | e   |
|-----|-----|-----|
| e   | e   | e   |
| e   | e   | e   | 

Press 1 to play next move, 

Press 2 to see position board

Press 3 to quit


=> 1

|-----|-----|-----|
| e   | e   | e   |
| e   | e   | e   |
